Who We Are

BUILT ON
ANALOGUE
SOUL.

EGO TV is a creative agency out of Johannesburg, South Africa. We shoot, direct, and build — photography, video, and web work for brands and artists who care about how things look and feel.

The name comes from the founder, Oge. EGO is OGE in reverse. That tension — between identity and expression, between the person behind the lens and the world in front of it — is what drives the work.

We're drawn to grain, warmth, and the imperfect beauty of analogue media. Everything we make carries that DNA.

Named to the DC100 List — DC Shoes & Creative Nestlings' Top 100 Creatives Pushing Youth Culture in South Africa, 2025.
